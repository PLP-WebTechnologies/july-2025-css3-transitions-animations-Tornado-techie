🌿 Healthy Lifestyle Tips

Hover or click inside sections to learn more.

🍎 Eat a Balanced Diet

Incorporate fruits, vegetables, proteins, whole grains, and healthy fats in your meals.

🏃‍♂️ Regular Physical Activity

Engage in at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily like walking, cycling, or dancing.

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink 6–8 glasses of water daily to support digestion, energy, and skin health.

🛏️ Get Quality Sleep

Aim for 7–9 hours of sleep each night for better mental and physical recovery.

🧠 Mental Wellness

Practice mindfulness, take breaks, meditate, or talk to a friend or counselor regularly.

🚭 Avoid Smoking and Limit Alcohol

Quit smoking and limit alcohol intake to reduce health risks.
